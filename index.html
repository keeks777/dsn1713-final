About Suzanne Collins

Award-winning author of The Hunger Games and The Underland Chronicles

Television Roots

Began her career writing for children's television shows on Nickelodeon, including Emmy-nominated series like Clarissa Explains It All.

Literary Success

Authored multiple bestselling series including The Underland Chronicles and The Hunger Games trilogy, which sold over 100 million copies worldwide.

Military Heritage

Daughter of a U.S. Air Force lieutenant colonel, her military background deeply influences her storytelling and themes of survival.
